About Us

GIVING POWER BACK TO WOMEN THROUGH HANDS ON SKILLS TRAINING.

Angola Smiles gives hope and a second chance to victims of gender based war by offering craft training.The women after the training are able to apply what they learnt and earn a living from it.

QUALITY HANDMADE ITEMS MADE BY THE GROUP

The women in the self help group make a wide variety beautiful items that can be sold both locally and globally. Profits from the sale of these items go into campaigns against oppression of women and towards supporting the many women in the group financially.
